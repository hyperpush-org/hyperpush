From language to ecosystem — a transparent look at where we've been, where we are, and where we're going.

01 Shipped
Forge the Language
Built Mesh from the ground up — a compiled, statically typed language with first-class actor concurrency, a built-in cluster runtime, and a package registry.
02 Shipped
Prove It in Production
Started building Hyperpush — an open-source error tracking platform powered by Mesh. If the language can't power its own apps, it's not ready for yours.
03 Now
Go Public at a Hackathon
Applying to major hackathons and officially open-sourcing Mesh under MIT. The full compiler, runtime, toolchain, and package registry — all public.
04 Upcoming
The Inaugural Mesh Hackathon
Our first community event — build packages, tools, or apps in Mesh and win real prizes. Top submissions earn a permanent Founding Builder badge on the registry.
05 Upcoming
Launch Hyperpush 1.0
Full public launch — open-source error tracking with AI root-cause analysis, Solana program monitoring, and automated bug bounties paid directly to developers.
06 Upcoming
Fund the Builders
Launch a recurring sponsorship program for open-source projects building on Mesh. Because great ecosystems don't survive on good intentions alone.
